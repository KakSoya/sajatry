ABOUT
XDINARY HEROES

Xdinary Heroes is a six‑member South Korean rock band formed under JYP Entertainment’s sub‑label, Studio J. Since their debut on December 6, 2021 with “Happy Death Day”, they’ve built a reputation for raw, instrument‑driven performances and emotionally honest songwriting . At their core, XH blends alternative rock, pop‑punk, metal, and emo, with every member—Gunil, Jungsu, Gaon, O.de, Jun Han, and Jooyeon—involved in writing, composing, and producing

Girl whose dreams are too beautiful to lose
져 버리기엔 너무 아름다운 꿈을 꾸는 소녀여

I love Dreaming Girl because it feels like a soft confession hidden in a melody. Unlike their usual bold, intense sound, this song has a gentler rhythm, almost like a lullaby for the heart. The lyrics speak of longing and admiration, of someone watching from afar and hoping their feelings will somehow reach the person they care about. There’s a bittersweet ache in it, the kind that reminds you of quiet crushes, late-night thoughts, and unspoken words. It makes me think of the people we hold close in our dreams but not in real life. And maybe that’s why it hits so deeply—it captures that quiet kind of love, the one that stays even when nothing is said out loud. Dreaming Girl isn’t loud, but it leaves a lasting echo in the heart.
