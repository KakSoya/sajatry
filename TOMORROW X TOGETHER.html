TOMORROW BY TOGETHER

TXT (Tomorrow X Together) is a South Korean boy group formed by BigHit Music (home of BTS) and debuted on March 4, 2019. The name Tomorrow X Together represents five individuals who come together under one dream, hoping to build a better tomorrow. Known for their genre-blending music, powerful storytelling, and youthful energy, TXT has quickly become one of the most influential K-pop groups of their generation.
